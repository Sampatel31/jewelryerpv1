Features

🧾

Billing & Invoicing

Create, print, and export professional gold jewellery invoices as PDF or Excel with GST calculations.

📦

Inventory Management

Track gold items by weight, purity, and HUID. Low-stock alerts and inter-branch transfers.

👥

Customer Management

Customer profiles, GSTIN validation, loyalty points, and full purchase history.

📊

Comprehensive Reports

Daily sales, customer statements, inventory reports, and GSTR-1 export.

📈

Gold Rate Engine

Automatic MCX rate scraping with manual override and full rate history.

🤖

AI Insights

ML.NET-powered sales forecasting, anomaly detection, and restock suggestions.

🔄

Multi-Shop Sync

Near-real-time sync engine between branches with automatic conflict resolution.

🔐

Security & RBAC

2FA OTP, role-based access control, and a complete audit trail for every action.

⚙️

Settings & Customisation

Company profile, tax rates, light/dark themes, and scheduled backup/restore.
